Avatar is a sci-fi film set in the mid-22nd century on the lush alien moon Pandora. Humans are mining a valuable resource there, but it threatens the native Na’vi people and their environment. The story follows Jake Sully, a paraplegic former Marine who is sent to Pandora to operate an “avatar” — a genetically engineered Na’vi body. Through this avatar, he can walk again and interact with the Na’vi. Jake is guided by Neytiri, a Na’vi woman who teaches him their culture and deep spiritual connection with nature. As he spends more time with them, Jake begins to sympathize with their way of life and falls in love with Neytiri.
